The Run with the Pack 5K will be held at Wake Med Cross Country Course in Cary, NC:

Venue information

Course: the Wake Med 5K course is one of the premier 5K courses of the southeast.

Parking: free parking will be available at Wake Med Cross Country Course behind the race finishing area. See the facility map.

Bathrooms: portapotties will be provided near the finishing area.

Course & facility map: click here to download the Wake Med facility map.

Address:

WakeMed Soccer Park Cross Country Course
201 Soccer Park Dr.
Cary, NC 27511

Directions:

From I-40 Eastbound (Chapel Hill/Durham)

  • From I-40 east, take exit 290 for NC-54 toward Cary. Turn right at the top of the exit ramp.
  • In 0.3 miles, turn left onto Trinity Rd.
  • Continue straight into Wake Med Cross Country Course. Parking will be on the right.

From I-40 Westbound (Fayetteville)

  • From I-40 west, take Exit 291 for NC-54 toward Cary. Turn left at the top of the exit ramp.
  • In 0.3 miles, turn left onto Trinity Rd.
  • Continue straight into Wake Med Cross Country Course. Parking will be on the right.

From Downtown Cary

  • Head east on Chatham St.
  • In 2 miles, turn left into Wake Med Cross Country Course.

From Raleigh/Beltline

  • From I-440 West, take exit 2B toward Cary and merge onto Western Blvd.
  • In 2.8 miles, turn left into Wake Med Cross Country Course.
